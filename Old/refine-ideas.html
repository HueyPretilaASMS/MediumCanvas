Refine Ideas

From page 29 of this website https://education.uky.edu/nxgla/wp-content/uploads/sites/33/2016/11/Designers-workbook.pdf

Value, Needs

-Lets artists collaborate across different countries

-Allows business to check up on design at any given time

-Allows more efficiency in melding styles and strengths by preventing to wait for another artist to finsih their section and also awkward transitions from cutting and pasting sections done in separate file

Challenges, Barriers

-Not enough experience with coding to launch program and ensure that it functions within time constraints

-Having to differentiate from other digital art userinterfaces such as Photoshop by create different designs

-Finding the most efficient way to enable real-time editing between more than one person
